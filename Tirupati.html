Explore Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh (AP) is known for its stunning beaches like Rishikonda Beach, iconic temples such as Kanaka Durga Temple and Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, and historical gems like the Amaravati Stupa. With a mix of natural beauty, religious significance, and ancient heritage, AP promises a captivating and memorable travel experience for visitors.

Day 1: Visakhapatnam - Beaches and Naval History

Morning: Arrive in Visakhapatnam, known for its beautiful beaches and naval history.

Day Activities: Start your morning with beach activities at RK Beach, enjoying volleyball, camel rides, and fresh seafood delights. Explore the INS Kursura Submarine Museum from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Take in panoramic views and recreational activities at Kailasagiri Hill Park, open from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, offering ropeway rides and serene gardens. Discover the region's heritage at Visakha Museum between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM


Explore Kailasagiri Hill:
Besides panoramic views, Kailasagiri offers a peaceful environment with lush greenery, children's play areas, and a ropeway for an exciting ascent or descent.

Visit Rishikonda Beach:
Known for its golden sands and clear waters, Rishikonda Beach is ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports like jet skiing and banana boat rides.

Day 2: Vijayawada - Temples and Cultural Heritage

Morning: Travel to Vijayawada, known for its religious sites and cultural heritage.

Day Activities: Begin your day with water sports and scenic views at Bhavani Island, followed by a visit to the ancient Undavalli Caves showcasing rock-cut architecture. Explore spiritual tranquility at Kanaka Durga Temple, a significant religious site open from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Indulge in Andhra cuisine at local eateries like Ramavarappadu Junction, known for its spicy biryanis and traditional thalis.


Visit the Kanaka Durga Temple:
Perched atop Indrakeeladri Hill, Kanaka Durga Temple is a major pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Durga. The temple offers breathtaking views of Vijayawada and is known for its grand architecture and religious significance.

Explore the Undavalli Caves:
These ancient rock-cut caves, located near Vijayawada, showcase exquisite sculptures, intricate carvings, and Buddhist artworks.

Day 3: Tirupati - Pilgrimage and Natural Beauty

Morning: Arrive in Tirupati,famous for its pilgrimage sites and natural attractions .

Day Activities: Immerse yourself in a day of spirituality and cultural discovery in Tirupati. Begin by visiting the Venkateswara Temple, renowned for its architectural grandeur, open from 5:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Enjoy the tranquil ambiance of Kapila Theertham, a scenic waterfall and temple complex perfect for relaxation and introspection. Delight your taste buds with authentic Andhra cuisine at Bhimas


Visit Tirumala:
Seek blessings at the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the holiest Hindu temples and visit surrounding temples by it

Explore Sri Vari Museum:
learn about the temple's history and architecture. Indulge in local cuisine and shopping experiences in Tirupati town.
